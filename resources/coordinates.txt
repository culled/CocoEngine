Coordinate system: Right-handed
	- X-axis = index finger
	- Y-axis = middle finger
	- Z-axis = thumb

+X = left
+Y = up
+Z = forward


	   (Y) (Z)
	    |  /
	    | /
	    |/
(X)---------+--------(-X)
	   /|
	  / |
	 /  |
       (-Z)(-Y)

Positive rotation = turn counter-clockwise when looking from the positive to negative direction on the axis

Rotation Matrix:
	[xx, yx, zx]
	[xy, yy, zy]
	[xz, yz, zz]
